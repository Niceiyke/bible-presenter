# Bible Presenter Keyboard Shortcuts

This document lists the available keyboard shortcuts for managing the projection and various processes in the Bible Presenter application.

## General Controls

- **ESC**: Clear Live Output immediately (blanks the screen).
- **Enter**: Go Live with the currently staged item.
- **Ctrl + O**: Toggle the Output Window (Show/Hide).
- **Ctrl + B**: Toggle Blackout (Stop projection without clearing item).
- **Ctrl + T**: Toggle Lower Third Deck (Show/Hide bottom dock).
- **Ctrl + S**: Toggle Settings Tab.

## Navigation & Tabs

- **F1**: Switch to **Bible** Tab.
- **F2**: Switch to **Songs** Tab.
- **F3**: Switch to **Media** Tab.
- **F4**: Switch to **PPTX Presentations** Tab.
- **F5**: Switch to **Custom Studio** Tab.

## Bible & Verses

- **N**: Stage the **Next Verse** in the sequence.
- **Ctrl + N**: Go Live with the **Next Verse** immediately.

## Presentations & Slides

- **Arrow Right**: Next Slide (PowerPoint or Custom Studio Slide).
- **Arrow Left**: Previous Slide (PowerPoint or Custom Studio Slide).

## Lower Thirds

- **Ctrl + Space**: Toggle Lower Third visibility (Show/Hide).
- **Page Down**: Next Lyric Line (when in Lyrics mode and Lower Third is visible).
- **Page Up**: Previous Lyric Line (when in Lyrics mode and Lower Third is visible).

## Media Controls (Live Output)

- **K**: Play / Pause live video.
- **R**: Restart live video (back to 00:00).
- **M**: Mute / Unmute live video audio.
